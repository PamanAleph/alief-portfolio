Two core modules of a cross-border marketplace, built and shipped end to end on the backend.

## Beliin — proxy purchasing

Lets a user buy multiple items from foreign marketplaces through a single order — the platform acts as the purchasing proxy, consolidating items that would otherwise mean separate checkouts, separate shipments, and separate risk.

## Kirimin — international shipping

Handles the logistics half: international shipping and customs-adjacent handling once items are bought, keeping the purchase and shipping legs of the transaction connected end to end.

## Supporting work

- Built and maintained backend services on Express.js and MongoDB, with real-time data synchronization — a **20%** cut in API response time.
- Integrated AI-powered auto-reply and a ticketing system into customer support, to keep response times down as order volume grew.

## Result

A **100%** client satisfaction rate over the engagement, with the transaction flow — purchase to delivery — running as one connected system instead of two handoffs.
